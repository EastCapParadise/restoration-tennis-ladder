Welcome

Restoration Tennis Ladder is a collection of local DMV tennis enthusiasts who want a simple, semi-competitive tennis ladder. This ladder was created from members of Restoration Anglican Church (not officially affliliated) and is open to any and all. We welcome anyone to join the ladder, challenge each other, organize a time to play, report scores, and climb the rankings.

This inaugral season will use one combined ladder that gives points for both singles and doubles results. So get out there and play!

The 2026 season starts April 17th and runs through November 1st.

How it works

Players sign up with their contact info and self-rating. Players then reach out to each otherto find a time and court to play. You can find contact information in the directory. Match results are then reported through the site, and points are added to a combined ladder ranking. Player ratings are dynamic throughout the season. Final rankings for men's, women's, and combined ladders are based on points accumulated throughout the season.

We’ll keep the scoring system relatively simple at first. You gain points by playing matches with players gaining more points if you win.
