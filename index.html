How It Works

  1. Sign up with your name, contact info, and self-rating.
  2. Find an opponent in the Rankings or Directory and schedule a match.
  3. Play and report your result — ratings and points update automatically.
  4. Climb the ladder — most points at season's end wins.

Singles and doubles both count. Open to all skill levels.
